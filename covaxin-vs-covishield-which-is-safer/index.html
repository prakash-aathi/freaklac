Covaxin VS Covishield – Difference between each other

Detailed about Covaxin:

Covaxin has been clinically shown to be effective in combating COVID-19, and it may also preventing mutant variants from spreading. While the vaccine is a traditional model and carries no side effects of reactogenic-side effects, research studies and data collected on vaccine have so far shown that in all vaccines, Covaxin brings a small number of side effects, such as fever, redness, cold or pain to the injection site.

Therefore, for someone who is in pain, or worried about side effects, choosing Covaxin may be the safest option, according to Dr. Kataria. It is also possible that side effects are milder, and make your vaccination experience safer.
Currently, Covaxin also has a shorter timeline of dosage, which means doses will be given outside of 4-6 weeks. One can also make the right decision, especially if they are committed to the future, or are at high risk for the dangers of COVID-19.

Those with a history or risk of hemorrhage(blood clotting) may also be advised to take Covaxin at present as a blood clot is a reported result of Covishield. However, it is still considered something ‘unusual’ .

Detailed about Covidshield:

Covishield has been one of the first approved vaccines and widely used in India, since January 2021. Although it is also a traditional vaccine, Dr. Kataria warns that young people, who prefer Oxford-Astrazeneca , often experience more serious side effects than those who receive Covaxin. Several reports also point out that Covishield shows a more immune response (hence, it makes way for more side effects).

The Covishield is also given primarily to the elderly or those with serious comorbidities. Those between the ages of 18-45 should also observe their health limits before registering for the vaccine.
However, talking about the benefits, Dr. Kataria added that Covishield puts in more antibodies, compared to Covaxin (although its performance levels are the same weight). Those with weakened immune systems may consider taking this action.
