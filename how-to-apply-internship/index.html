what is an internship?

According to me, an internship is a limited period of time , working experience offered by an employer is given to students.

I classified internship into two types paid and voluntary internships.
The trend is now voluntary internships, they lot of organization offered voluntary internships.
As coming to paid internships, it is usually a lot competitive to select that internship. Yep! this is a competitive world. Don’t worry I give some ideas and tricks to get paid internships.. keep reading…

how to apply for an internship?

Basically there following different methods in various organization…let see detailed about.

  • The high-level organization require to fill out an online application form.
  • sometimes they said to send an application through a general email address.
  • some organization says submit your resume or cv through the website portal.

As a beginner, you fealing hard to find a good internship but my suggestion is to try ‘Internshala it’s an open free platform. I explained step by step how to get an internship through Internshala.

what is Internshala?

Internshala is India’s big internship platform, it connects the organization looking for interns and students who are interested in the internship and, with 40000+ paid internships in Engineering, MBA, media, law, arts, and other streams.

how to apply for an internship through Internshala?

how to log in Internshala?

if you already signup Internshala then ignore this step move to next.

Let see details about this step by step. first, click here it redirects to the Internshala website. Then you find the register button click that it shows two options select “As a Student “

Next step you will enter email, the first name, last name and password then click signup. After that, it sends a verification link through Gmail and verified it.

After verify your email it will redirects to personal details page.

Enter your details, and make sure given correct phone number, some organization make telephone interview. and hit the save & next button.

then next page shows preferences select ”Internships” after that it shows internship type looking for work from home or office . just select Work from home because in this covid situation many organizations preferred WFH .

Next below you find which field is looking for an internship. select your interested internship. I give some basic field which all works have basic English knowledge Digital marketing, marketing, sales, proofreading, social media marketing, transcription/ translation, campus ambassador, event management, data entry, teaching, volunteering. Then hit the save button.

after this step completed , you ready to find internship.

how to find an internship?

The dashboard shows a lot of internships in work from home. Normally first in the dashboard, it shows ongoing contest or online training skills, basically, it will be paid. After that, it shows a lot of free internships, scroll down, and see many opportunities.

But now we going to use filters, it saves maximum amount of time.

Go on category, then select your interested internships Make sure choose at least 5. if you have not aware of these skills then choose a basic internship, if you have some basic English knowledge definitely rock on that internships.

click the work from home toolbar and leave as it as stipend range zero. Refer the image below.

How to check a genuine internship?

  • Pay attention to sources
  • Do some research
  • Look at requirements
  • Look for compensation details

How to add Education details in Internshala?

If you are adding more details to your resume will increase your chances of getting selected. so fill in all possible entry. And also add accurate details.

If you are currently pursuing any degree then fill in college name, degree and stream name and also CGPA or percentage.

Add your past study details 10th and 12th grade details too…

How to add Experience, skills, work samples in internshala ?

If you have worked previously any jobs then fill it in jobs and if you have completed any internships fill it in internship column. Most of the organization is looking for experienced person.

next skill, there is a real trick the organization got a lot of applications you want to be unique, Don’t put unrelated skills on that. For example, the company is related to web development then search google for what type of project is doing, they’re using WordPress or static pages. Then put on the skills they looking for.

But in most case we didn’t have any experience, then what you going to do is make your project and link into the Internshala. Let see how to do this. First make your project, if its project is related to web development make your own site and link it or if you good at coding then link it to your git hub account or if you are good at designing then link it to your Behance Portfolio or else if your project is not in this category, then share your project through google drive.

After all this completed it will generate your resume then you click proceed to application.

How to answer basic interview questions?

Why should you be hired for this role?

  • Highlight your strengths – mention any experience (internship/job experience or extra-curricular activities), accomplishments, skills that are relevant to the role.
  • Show enthusiasm – mention what excites you about this role and the company. You can go through their website/ social media to understand what interests you
  • Do not copy answers from the internet.

Are you available for 2 month, starting immediately, for a full-time work from home internship?

These type of question answer your available time clearly.

  • For the prescribed two month internship I would like to extend my joining immediately.
  • I am available to work in your esteemed from this point in time. As this internship is home-based I would like to start the internship at this regular point in time.
  • To start new can we start by the next month starting as there are only 2 days left for the next month to begin and looking forward to hearing from you soon

Conclusion

Try as much possible to apply internships at regular interval. Don’t afraid of failures keep trying. Then it becomes experienced to answer the interview questions and keep at practicing on real projects. Feel free to contact me to clarify any doubt.
